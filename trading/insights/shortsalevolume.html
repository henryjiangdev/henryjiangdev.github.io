Short Sale Volume


Description: Gather data that showed short volume per company. Then added up all the short volume and total volume. This data set is all short sale volume and total volume of each day.

Graph

Showing how many shorts initiated compared to longs. Many times they move together, this is most likely from HFTs/Market Makers, transacting orders. Some interesting points are when they move in opposite directions, which seems to mean someone is buying or shorting more in the overall market.

Difference of the two columns, shows long bias in the long run. The 2020 crash starting February 20, 2020 is the small spike to the left. Currently we are in a weird situation where thre is more buying when price is near its all time highs. Can be caused by interest rates rising. Recently interest rates have risen to pre-crash levels.

The difference seems to stabalize between 0.25billion shares or a small short bias throughout the entire days.
